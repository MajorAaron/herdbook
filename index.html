Features

Everything your flock needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built for hobby farmers who care about their animals and want to breed smarter, not harder.

Animal Registry

Add every animal with breed, photos, band or tag numbers, birthdate, and notes. Filter and search your whole flock in seconds.

Lineage Trees

Visual pedigree charts up to 5 generations deep. See exactly who begat whom and spot inbreeding risks before they happen.

Breeding Log

Record pairings, track incubation and gestation periods, and get reminders when hatch day or kidding is approaching.

Health Records

Track vaccinations, treatments, vet visits, and weight over time. Set reminders for recurring treatments so nothing slips through.

Hatch & Kidding Tracker

Log clutch sizes, hatch rates, and mortality. Track which pairings produce the healthiest offspring over time.

AI Breeding Assistant

Ask questions about your flock and get genetics-informed breeding suggestions. "Which rooster gives the best olive eggers?"
